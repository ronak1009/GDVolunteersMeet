content_paste Objective of the Meet

With the blessings of Lord Neminath and our Guru Acharya Shri Dharmarakshit M.S. and Acharya Shri Hemvallabhsuriswarji M.S. we are organizing a volunteer's meet for all those who are interested to contribute for promoting and doing seva of Girnar Maha Tirth. Our vision is to create a platform for anyone to contribute from anywhere, by using their professional skills and talent. This shall not only enable us to unite a force for Girnarji but also inspire devotion towards Girnarji and Lord Neminath Dada in our hearts.

The objective of the meet is to brainstorm different ideas on how we can promote Girnarji across different mediums (digital platform in form of social media, ground work at Girnarji, etc..) We expect that volunteers come up with new ideas during this meet and be open to collaborate with others to create an impact for Girnarji.

Jai Girnar, Jai Neminath !

new_releases Terms and Conditions

  1. Travel arrangements are to be done by the volunteers themselves. Volunteers should arrive at Girnar Darshan dharamshala on 01/06/2018 by 9:00 AM.
  2. Registrations are non-transferrable. No one except for the registered volunteer shall be allowed to participate in the volunteer meet.
  3. Due to high interest, registrations is based on first come, first serve basis. Incase the capacity is full, you shall be in waitlist. If you don't move from waitlist to confirmed list, the registration amount shall be refuned to you by 15/06/2018.
  4. The meals during the meet shall be taken care by Summer Navanny Sangh at Girnar Darshan Dharamshala. However it encourgaded to contribute in the Bhojanshala / Annukampa funds at Girnar Darshan dharamshala office.
  5. Volunteers shall have to follow the schedule of the meet strictly to ensure the objective of the meet is fulfilled.
  6. Volunteers need to do Chovihar and follow basic principles of Jainism.
  7. Volunteers are expected to follow discipline and Girnar Darshan dharamshala can take appropriate actions for any misconduct of any volunteers during the meet.
  8. This is a volunteer meet for Girnar Tirth Seva, it is expected that the volunteers co-operate with each other and organizers to make this event successfull and memorable.
